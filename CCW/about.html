About Concentraining

Concentraining is affiliated with Tae son Martial artsThis means that our students' rank certifications are not nationally and internationally recognized. They are however recognized locally and will be taken into consideration when joining the Tae Son Martial Arts dojo.

We teach the four major components of Tae Kwon Do those are Forms,Sparring,Self-Defense,and kicking techniques.

No other form of physical activity can offer Coordination, flexibility, weight loss, concentration, increased self-confidence and leadership skills. This art is designed to use speed and skill, not bulk strength, so all of us regardless of age or gender can participate together. Young students, teenager and adults will learn to focus their energy in positive ways that carry over outside the school.
